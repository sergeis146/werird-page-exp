The Fascinating World of Butterflies

Their Amazing Life Cycle

Butterflies undergo a remarkable transformation, known as metamorphosis, during their life cycle. It's a process of incredible change, starting as a tiny egg, then hatching into a hungry caterpillar, forming a chrysalis, and finally emerging as a beautiful butterfly.

Interesting Facts

Butterflies taste with their feet! They have taste receptors on their feet that help them find the best plants to lay their eggs on.
The lifespan of a butterfly varies greatly, with some species living for only a few weeks, while others can live for several months.
Butterflies are important pollinators, helping to fertilize plants and ensure the survival of many ecosystems.
